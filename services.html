Other Services

There are more than 60 immigration programs available to individuals seeking to immigrate to Canada. Most of them are mentioned in broad category while other immigration options are also available. A few of those programs are listed below:

  • Atlantic Immigration Pilot (AIPP)
  • Rrual and Nothern Immigration Pilot (RNIP)
  • PR card renewal
  • Canadian Citizenship
  • Business Immigration
  • Entrepreneur Programs
  • Start Up Visa
  • Investor Programs
  • Self Employed Programs
  • Owner Operator LMIA
  • Regional Pilot
  • Farm Category
  • International Graduate
  • Home Child Care Provider Pilot and Home Support Worker Pilot
